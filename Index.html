Top 10 Favorite Movies of All Time

  • In the Mood for Love (2000)

    A romantic drama that explores the themes of memory and regret, set in 1960s Hong Kong.

  • Chungking Express (1994)

    A quirky romantic comedy that follows two cops and their respective love interests in Hong Kong.

  • Days of Being Wild (1990)

    A romantic drama that explores the themes of love, loss, and identity in 1960s Hong Kong.

  • The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

    A highly acclaimed drama about hope, redemption, and the power of the human spirit.

  • The Godfather (1972)

    A crime drama that explores the world of organized crime and the moral consequences of the choices we make.

  • The Dark Knight (2008)

    An action-packed superhero thriller that explores the themes of heroism and sacrifice.

  • 12 Angry Men (1957)

    A gripping courtroom drama that examines the complexities of justice and the human condition.

  • Forrest Gump (1994)

    A heartwarming drama that follows the extraordinary life of a simple man with a big heart.

  • The Lion King (1994)

    An animated classic that tells the story of a young lion's journey to reclaim his throne.

  • Spirited Away (2001)

    A visually stunning animated film that takes the viewer on a magical journey through a fantastical world.
